About Kabwe Chamber

The Kabwe Chamber of Commerce and Industry is a non-profit association promoting the interests of businesses in Kabwe. We provide advocacy, networking, and resources to support sustainable economic growth.

  • Networking Opportunities
  • Economic Development
  • SME Support & Training
